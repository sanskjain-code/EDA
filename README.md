# Sales Dataset - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a sales dataset using Python. The goal is to understand sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and relationships between different variables through visualizations.

## Tools & Libraries Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

## Dataset Columns

* Order_ID
* Order_Date
* Customer_ID
* Customer_Name
* Age
* Gender
* City
* Product
* Category
* Quantity
* Unit_Price
* Total_Sales

## EDA Analyses Performed

### 1. Monthly Sales Trend

Analyzed how sales changed over time.

### 2. Top Selling Products

Identified products generating the highest revenue.

### 3. Category-wise Sales Contribution

Compared sales contribution of different categories.

### 4. City-wise Sales Distribution

Analyzed sales performance across cities.

### 5. Customer Age Distribution

Studied the age range of customers.

### 6. Gender Distribution

Compared male and female customer proportions.

### 7. Average Order Value by Category

Calculated average sales value for each category.

### 8. Quantity vs Unit Price

Explored the relationship between product price and quantity sold.

### 9. Top Repeat Customers

Identified customers with the highest number of orders.

### 10. Correlation Heatmap

Examined relationships among numerical variables.

## Key Insights

* Electronics contributed the highest share of total sales.
* Laptop and Mobile were among the best-performing products.
* Sales remained relatively stable throughout the year.
* Customer distribution was nearly balanced across genders.
* Different cities contributed significantly to overall revenue.
* Quantity and Unit Price showed a weak relationship.
